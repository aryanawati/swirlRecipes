Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 3 tablespoons of milk
  • 3 teaspoons of chocolate chips
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Get a medium-large cup, and add all your dry ingredients except chocolate chips.
  2. Stir properly with a fork to get rid of clumps.
  3. Add your wet ingredients, and stir until it becomes thick and gooey.
  4. Add your chocolate chips, and stir them a bit more, not breaking the chips.
  5. Heat in the microwave for 1-2 mins depending on microwave wattage.
  6. Stick a toothpick in the center once it is finished, if batter sticks to the pick, put it back in the microwave for 15-30 seconds.
  7. Allow it to cool, top with chocolate chips, and enjoy!
