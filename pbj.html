Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread (White or Wheat, up to you)
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly/Jam (Grape is ideal, but up to you)
  • Peanut Butter

Instructions

  1. Lay two slices of bread on a clean surface or plate.
  2. Use a butter knife to spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  3. Use another butter knife (don't cross contaminate) to spread jelly on another slide of bread.
  4. Place the slices ontop of each other so that the spread's face each other.
  5. Cut the sandwich to liking (in-half, diagonally, squares) and enjoy immediately.
